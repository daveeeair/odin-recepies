Wraps

Description

Well, for starters, this crispy chicken wrap is healthier than the usual.
The chicken is baked and not fried, there is no oil needed to cook the chicken. It’s a simple, flavorful breading that sticks so nicely to the chicken breast.
This recipe comes together quickly and it’s very easy to follow.
The entire family will love this for lunch or for dinner.
Change up the flavors with different sauce combos like Spicy Honey Mustard, Creamy Pesto Dressing, Chili Onion Crunch.

Ingredients

  • Wraps
  • Chicken Breasts
  • Egg
  • Mayo
  • Sakt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Set oven 220º C
  2. Place the egg, mayo, salt, garlic powder, and pepper in a medium bowl and whisk until smooth; set aside.
  3. Get a baking sheet out and place a wire rack/cooling rack on it. (This is the trick to getting the chicken perfectly crispy and cooked.)
  4. Mix panko and seasoning together.
  5. Add it to a skillet and toast on medium/low heat, stirring often.
  6. Only toast until the panko darkens a bit in color, about 5 minutes. Take it off the heat and set aside.
  7. Working with one chicken breast at a time, place inside a gallon zip-top bag and pound with the flat side of a meat mallet or rolling pin to an even 1/4-inch thickness. Set the flattened chicken breast aside and repeat with remaining breasts.
  8. Drudge the chicken in the egg/mayo wash and then coat both sides with the panko crumb (I just coat the chicken in the skillet I used to taste the panko).
  9. Place the coated breasts on the wire rack thats on the baking sheet. Bake in the oven for 17-20 minutes or until internal temp is 75º C.
  10. Once chicken is cooked, let it rest for a few minutes before slicing up.
  11. lice up lettuce, tomato and get the mayo mixture and cheese ready.
  12. Assemble the wrap. Place all toppings and chicken into the center of the wrap, try not to overfill.
  13. Drizzle sauce of choice over the ingredients.
  14. Fold the wrap up like you would a burrito, both ends tucked in and then rolled horizontally.
  15. Heat up oiled skillet pan on medium/high heat.
  16. Place the wrap with the folded side down first to seal it. Let it cooked untouched for about 4 minutes each side.
  17. Once it is browned and crispy, remove from heat and let it cool for a minute.
  18. Then eat and enjoy.
